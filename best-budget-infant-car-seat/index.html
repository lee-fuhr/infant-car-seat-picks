For the best budget infant car seat that doesn't cut corners on safety, the Evenflo Tribute Lite Convertible Car Seat is your straightforward pick, it’s light, affordable, and has a loyal following among parents who just need a dependable seat.

If you prioritize portability, the Cosco Scenera NEXT is the lightweight champion for travel and small cars; if cabin space is tight, the Graco SlimFit’s compact profile makes it the top choice for fitting multiple seats across the back row.

Shopping for a convertible car seat on a budget means you’re already making a smart, long-term move: one seat that grows with your child from rear-facing infancy to forward-facing toddlerhood. The catch is that budget constraints can make it tempting to overlook crucial fit and ease-of-use details. The good news? There are several affordable models that earn strong safety ratings from independent reviewers and consistent praise from parents, you don’t have to spend a fortune to get a seat that installs securely and keeps your little one comfortable. When choosing among the best budget infant car seats, focus on three things: the weight-and-height limits for rear-facing (the longer, the better), the ease of installing it tightly with your vehicle’s seat belts or LATCH system, and the seat’s overall dimensions relative to your car’s back seat. The three seats here, Evenflo Tribute Lite, Cosco Scenera NEXT, and Graco SlimFit, each excel in one specific area while making smart compromises elsewhere. Read on to see which trade-offs align with your priorities.

Best for specific needs

Best for tight budgets

If your primary goal is spending as little as possible while still getting a certified, safe convertible seat, the Evenflo Tribute Lite is the clear winner. It’s light enough to move between cars easily and has a proven track record with owners. Just be prepared for a bare-bones experience, no extra padding or easy-adjust harness, but for the price, it’s a reliable workhorse. Our pick: Evenflo Tribute Lite Convertible Car Seat.

Best for travel and portability

The Cosco Scenera NEXT is the lightest and most travel-friendly seat we researched. It folds down flat for storage and is easy to carry through an airport or toss into a trunk. While it skips comforts like cup holders and thick padding, it excels where it counts: being small, light, and safe. It’s also a top pick among frequent fliers and ride-share parents. Our pick: Cosco Scenera NEXT Convertible Car Seat.

Best for small cars and tight three-across setups

When rear-seat real estate is at a premium, the Graco SlimFit’s narrow width is a game-changer. It fits easily next to other seats or boosters, and the no-rethread harness makes adjusting as your child grows a breeze. The slightly higher cost is offset by longer rear-facing limits and better everyday usability. It’s the premium budget choice for space-conscious families. Our pick: Graco SlimFit Convertible Car Seat.

Our verdict

Choosing between these three comes down to your specific needs. For the absolute lowest cost and good portability, grab the Evenflo Tribute Lite. If you’re a travel-heavy family, the featherlight Cosco Scenera NEXT will be your best companion. And when space is the limiting factor, whether you drive a compact car or need to fill the back row, the Graco SlimFit offers the best combination of narrowness, convenience, and longevity for just a bit more money. All three are safe, all three are affordable, and any one of them will serve your family well.
